1. What is unique about these tests?

The tests follows the open book method.

2. Who all can take the tests?

Any one above standard eight can take the tests.



3. Are there any age criteria for the tests?

There is no upper age limit.

4. Are there any prizes for the winners?

Absolutely there are many prizes and consolations prizes for the winners. The participants will also be issued the participation e-certificates.



5. Can I use any resource during the test?

Yes, the candidate can use any resource of their choice.

6. What if someone else gives the test instead of the registered applicant?

If it is found that some other person is giving the test instead of the registered applicant, the company has the right to cancel the registration and block the applicant from applying again.



7. Is there any refund policy?

The only possibility of refund is when the organization itself cancels the test due to some reason. The reason would be communicated to the applicants and the amount paid would be refunded.

8. Is any personal id required while claiming the prize amount?

Yes, The winner is supposed to provide any of the personal id's like Aadhar card/Passport/PAN card.



9. Can an applicant who has appeared for one series of test apply again when the test is conducted for the next series?

Yes, an applicant is eligible for applying the tests every time it is conducted.

10. What if an applicant has registered but unable to give the test due to some unavoidable reasons?

The refund is not possible at this stage. However, it is at the company's discretion whether to allow the person to appear for the next series of the test without any registration.
